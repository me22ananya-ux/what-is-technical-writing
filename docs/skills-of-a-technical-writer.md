# Skills of a Technical Writing
There are several skills of a technical writer, let's have a look at some of the most crucial ones:
1. **Research**
- Research is one of the most crucial skills. It gathers information from various sources like subject-matter experts, technical documents, and external resources.
2. **Clarity**
- Clarity is to convert complex technical information into easy to understand language. A technical writer must avoid jargon and use simple language to make your documentation accessible to a wider audience.
3. **Audience Awareness**
- Understanding your target audience is crucial in creating content that meets their needs. Whether the content is for freshers or experts, it must be tailored to their requirement.
4. **Collaboration**
- Collaboration is a key skill as technical writers generally work with product developers, engineers, designers, and other stakeholders. They gather data from various team members and incorporate their feedback into the documentation.
5. **Tools**
- Technical writer should be proficient with various tools for writing, editing, and publishing documentation such as Microsoft Word, Google Docs, Markdown, MadCap Flare and Adobe FrameMaker.
